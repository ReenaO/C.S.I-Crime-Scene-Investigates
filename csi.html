FACTS:

C.S.I is not shot in Las Vegas

C.S.I is shot using skylines and sets in Santa Clarita, Calfornia and surrounding area. Occasionally filming in Las Vegas.

The C.S.I's are scientists not members of Police Department.

To Introduce C.S.I Miami a cross-jurisation episode was used.

A case starting in Las Vegas led them to Miami. As Miami team introduced themselves to Las Vegas team the audience was getting to know them.

Theme Tune is "Who are you?

The new series has one season film so far and there 10 episodes in the season. Sara sidle and Gil Grissom appear in 6 episodes.

William Peterson as Gil Grissom:

Is a forensic entomologist and has a doctoral degree in biology from University Of California. Gil was the shift supervisor on graveyard. He became a C.S.I in 1985 and left Las Vegas Crime lab in 2009. He was a researcher for a short time. Gil became a sea advocate and rejoined ex wife Sara. They sail off together to the Port of San Diego.

Marg Helgenberger as Catherine Willows:

Catherine is a blood splatter analyst. She is assistant supervisor on graveyard shift. She worked for a while as graveyard supervisor. Catherine was demoted after a departmental scandel and join F.B.I as a special agent.In the series finale Catherine returns and gets directorship of the crime lab.

Gary Dourdan as Warrick Brown:

Warrick is a csi level 3. He is an audio-video analyst and majorer in chemistry at University of Nevada. Warrick is a recovering gambler. He was accused and acquitted of murder. He was shot in his car by the under sheriff Jeffery McKeen. Warrick died from the gunshot in Grissoms arms.

George Eads as Nick Stokes:

Nick is a C.S.I level 3. Nick graduated from Texas A and M and joined Dallas police before moving to Las Vegas.He later became assistant night superviser under Catherine. Nick gets demoted back to level 3. Nick moves to San Siegoafter he gets the directorship of the SDPD Crime Lab.

Jorja Fox as Sara Sidle:

Sara is a materials and elements analyst. Sara majored in physics At Harvard University. At grissom request she moves from San Francisco to Las Vegas. Grissom and Sara later get married. After a turbulent relationship they get divorced. she gets promoted to director but gives it up to reunit with ex-husband Grissom. Catherine takes over as lab director.

Eric Szmanda as Greg Sanders:

Greg is a DNA Specialist. He graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Stanford. He joined the LVPD after a short time with SFPD. He wrote a book about the History of Las Vegas. Greg believes in psychic powers. Greg has many love interests.

Paul Guilfoyle as LVPD Captain Jim Brass:

Brass starts out as CSI team superviser on graveyard shift. He gets moved to homicide detective when rookie Holly Gribbs gets shot on her first night on the job. Jim is the detective who does most of the arrests and interrogations for the CSI. He later retires from the force to focus on his daughter. he takes a job as head of security at Catherine casino the Eclipse.

Robert David Hall as Dr. Albert "Al" Robbins:

Dr. Robbins is the chief medical examiner. He is the head county coroner of the LVPD. He lost his leg after get hit by a drunk driver as a teenager. he has a prosthetic leg. He is married with 3 children. He forms strong bonds with Gil Grissom and Raymond Langston. He very rarely leave the crime lab.he preforms autopsies and sends samples for forensic analysis.
